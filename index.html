Roller Coaster Rules:

  1. Obey the listed age, height, weight, and health restrictions.
  2. Watch the ride first, so you know what to expect.
  3. Never be afraid to ask the park employee questions.
  4. Keep you head, hands, arms legs and feet inside the ride at all times.
  5. Keep your eyes looking forward and your head up to protect your neck from injury.
  6. Wait until instructed to remove your safety belt/lap bars/shoulder harnesses and exit the ride.
  7. Take frequent breaks between roller coaster rides.
  8. Never stand up while a ride is in motion.*
